# A reservation & management system for clinics and hospitals
## Required features
* Accept pre-visit online couselling reservation
    * Set open timeframe for reservation & let the patient choose preferred slot
    * The patient needs to pay first before they can make a counselling appointment
    * Automatically assign a vacant counsellor from a pool of counsellors on shift
    * booking confirmation email
    * send a questionnaire the patient should fill in
    * Three roles on the calendar:
        * counsellor: normal staff. Can only do the following:
            * be assigned to a counselling appointment.
            * view the information on assigned patient.
            * add & edit information/data about the patient.
            * start a meeting.
            * send messages/emails to the patient
        * managers:
            * counsellors' capabilities
            * make changes to an appointment (appointment time, assigned counsellor)
            * see all patients' info
        * patient: Can only do the following
            * Make payment & book a slot
* Manage customer info securely
    * all info is sensitive, so privacy is important
* Multilingual
    * Make the app available at least in Japanese and English
